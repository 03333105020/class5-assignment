WHAT IS AN APPLE?

An apple is a round, edible fruit produced by an apple tree. More info about apple is written below.


Apples are a popular and versatile fruit known for their crisp texture and sweet to tart flavor. Originating from Central Asia, they come in numerous varieties, each with unique characteristics, such as the tart Granny Smith or the sweet Fuji. Rich in vitamins, fiber, and antioxidants, apples are not only a healthy snack but also a staple in various culinary dishes, from pies to salads. Their cultural significance spans centuries, symbolizing everything from knowledge to temptation, making them a beloved fruit around the world.
